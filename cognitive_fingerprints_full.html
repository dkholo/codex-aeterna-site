Key Discoveries

Hamlet = Bible

Both dominated by 9:00 (West). "Lord" and "King" anchor both texts. Shakespeare's tragedy is structurally a covenant drama — authority in crisis.

Musk = Book of the Dead

Both share heavy 11:00 presence. "Great/good" mirrors "gods/great." Separated by 4,000 years, they use superlatives as cognitive pillars.

Paradise Lost Stands Alone

6 of 10 hubs at 10:00. Milton's mind orbits pure vertical hierarchy — heaven, god, high. No other text concentrates like this.

The Novel Emerges

Don Quixote invented the modern pattern: character names as structural anchors. Monte Cristo and War and Peace follow the same template.
