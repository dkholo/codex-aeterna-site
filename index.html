What is a soul portrait?

Soul signatures & cognitive fingerprints

Every person thinks differently — not just in what they say, but in the structural patterns beneath their words. A soul portrait is the visual representation of these patterns: the unique way a mind connects ideas, revisits themes, and weaves language. We call it a consciousness signature because, like a fingerprint, no two are alike. From ancient scriptures to modern podcasts, every text reveals the hidden geometry of the mind that created it.
